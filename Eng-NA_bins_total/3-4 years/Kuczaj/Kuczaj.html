Project Description

This corpus consists of 210 files containing the diary study (1973–75) of Stan Kuczaj’s son, Abe. From age 2;4 to 4;1, two 30 minutes sessions of Abe’s spontaneous speech were recorded each week in the home. From 4;1 to 5;0, one 30-minute sample was taken weekly.
